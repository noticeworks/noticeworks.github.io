Hawaiian Airlines

The new HawaiianAirlines.com focuses on building vacation itineraries (not just buying airline tickets) while showcasing the company’s unrivaled knowledge of Hawaii.

Services

  • Design Research
  • Consumer Research
  • Prototyping
  • User Experience Design
  • Content Strategy
  • Front End Development

...an improved way to view and compare pricing and seating options and a smoother way to book hotels in addition to flights.

Rethinking the Booking Process, Hawaiian-Style

Power Booking – From searching for flights to reviewing itineraries to selecting seats, it’s an e-commerce experience that sells itineraries instead of just airplane tickets.

Selling Vacations – Leisure travelers spend a lot of time planning their dream vacation. We combined rich content with a simple, consistent booking widget to help users craft the perfect trip.

Streamlined Booking – For business travelers who crave efficiency, our booking widget offers a clear and direct way for them to quickly create itineraries.

Visitors can switch between the calendar view and a price chart to find the lowest available fares.

Research & Strategy: Understanding a Unique Industry

We analyzed internal data, interviewed key stakeholders, and helped translate Hawaiian's business strategy into a design strategy.

Selling Itineraries, Not Tickets

By focusing on building itineraries, we create opportunities to support the user’s travel needs without slowing the booking process.

A seamless customer journey enables travelers to create custom vacations as they move through the site.

As individuals build their intineraries, we present them with relevant add-ons such as transportation.

Inspiring Travelers with Compelling Content

We created an Island Guide for visitors to gain a sense of Hawaii's magic.

Visitors can explore the sites and activities of individual islands, gathering ideas as they plan their adventures.

It's an e-commerce platform that truly captures the spirit of Hawaii.

The new ‘hyper-focused’ look is all about making the navigation process easier and more intuitive, paired with beautiful imagery reflecting Hawaii’s authentic beauty.
